Game's instruction:
-   To place a card on a board, select a path card (by clicking on player's hand),
    and then click the board where the card supposed to be placed
-   The path card placed must be connected to the main path and not interfere with other part (because path placement
    logic is already completed and implemented)
-   To discard a card, click on a card you wish to discard and then click the trash bin icon to the right of player's
    hand
